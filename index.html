About us

The AL-BAIK story began in 1974 in the vibrant city of Jeddah, in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It was here that the late Shakour Abu Ghazalah recognized a growing demand for high-quality, affordable food served with speed and courtesy in a clean and welcoming environment. With a vision and determination, Shakour invested all his hard-earned savings into this venture, laying the foundation for what would become AL-BAIK. Since then, we have proudly maintained his commitment to excellence, serving delicious meals that bring people together. Our legacy continues to thrive, driven by a passion for quality and a dedication to our customers. Join us on this flavorful journey and experience the joy of AL-BAIK!.
